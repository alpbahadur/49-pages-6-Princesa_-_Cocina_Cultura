Our Story

A Journey Through Time

Nestled in the heart of Old San Juan's historic Paseo de la Princesa, Princesa - Cocina Cultura offers a unique culinary experience that transports you to 19th-century Puerto Rico.

We are the only restaurant crafting dishes from authentic Puerto Rican cookbooks dating from 1859 to 1950. Each recipe tells a story, each flavor captures a moment in our island's rich history.

Surrounded by over 20 trees and bonded to a historic wall, our magical garden setting creates an enchanting atmosphere where history and nature intertwine.

The Rum Library

Discover one of the most extensive collections of Puerto Rican rums in the world. Our library features rare vintage bottles, artisanal distillations, and contemporary masterpieces.

Each rum tells the story of Puerto Rico's sugar cane heritage, from colonial times to the present day. Our expert mixologists craft cocktails that honor these traditions while pushing the boundaries of modern gastronomy.

Finca La Zafra

Our farm, your table

At Princesa, we believe in knowing where our food comes from. That's why we maintain our own agroecological farm, Finca La Zafra, where we grow herbs, vegetables, and fruits using sustainable practices.

Our commitment to farm-to-table dining means you're always enjoying the freshest ingredients, harvested at peak ripeness and delivered directly to our kitchen.

We also partner with local farmers and producers who share our values, supporting Puerto Rico's agricultural community and ensuring authentic flavors in every dish.
